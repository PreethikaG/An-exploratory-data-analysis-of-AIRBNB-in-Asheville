Hello and welcome to my website that, through some visualizations, interactive plots, explores Airbnb data. I have primarily focussed on Asheville data, as I live in North Carolina. I have detailed the entire process I followed, from sourcing the data,cleaning, analyzing,visualizing the data in this website. Please feel free to checkout!

Following are a few questions that I wanted to address through my analysis:
  • How is the growth of Airbnb in Asheville?
  • What are the different types of properties available and how do they vary by neighborhood?
  • What neighbourhoods are highly rated by guests?
  • How do prices of listings vary by neighbourhoods?
  • How does seasonality affect prices of listings?
  • What features make a host Super host?
  • Which features of a listing are important for guests by analyzing the reviews from guests using Machine Learning?
Source: The dataset was sourced from http://insideairbnb.com/get-the-data.html

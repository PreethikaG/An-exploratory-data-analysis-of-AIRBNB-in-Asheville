The reviews_details.csv had a column called 'Comments' which included user's review of the listing and their experience of stay,etc. In order to find out which factors are more liked by the user's, I decided to generate a word cloud based on the reviews. From the word cloud, it can be found that the factors like cleanliness,comfort,host behaviour,etc are important from a user perspective.

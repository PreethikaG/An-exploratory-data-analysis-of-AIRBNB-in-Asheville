Who are superhosts?

According to Airbnb,superhosts are experienced hosts who provide a shining example for other hosts, and extraordinary experiences for their guests.

How can a host become a Superhost?

A host is a superhost if the following are met:
  • Completed at least 10 trips OR completed 3 reservations that total at least 100 nights
  • Maintained a 90% response rate or higher
  • Maintained a 1% percent cancellation rate
  • Maintained a 4.8 overall rating
